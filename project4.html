For the spring quarter at UCSD, I took COGs 187a class (Cognitive aspect of digital design). The last project of this class is to realize a website. So, I thought about a theme, which could be 'Travel in Europe' (See the step 1).

Step 1.Theme and Sketch (home page)

The theme could be 'Travel in Europe'. I thought to choose 5 or 6 countries, like for instance France, Germany, Italy. There will be three parts for each country: 1- local cuisine 2- go out 3- movies and books. You know it could be a practical guide to travel in Europe. And another idea will be to create a form, which will use to share good place, good recipes or something like that.
