Home > Portfolio > Project 2

For the spring quarter at UCSD, I took COGs 187a class (Cognitive aspect of digital design). The main goal of the first project was to create our own digital portfolio. It should have, at the least, a gallery with our different projects and one page for each project (presenting the different steps).

Step 1. Research and Sketch

Before this assignment we had to find 10 interesting portfolios and said what should be better, or what is good? So, this first project gave me the opportunity to explore the different layouts, designs. Then, I realized a sketch in order to organize my ideas. But you will see this one was just a sketch...

Step 2. Home page

At the beginning I didn't use the grid system. So, 2 days after I started again to create my home page with the grid system. But you know it's really easier... I chose a simple layout for my home page: a banner with the main parts, a picture, and a description of me.

Step 3. Contact Page

Nothing exciting to say. It's just my contact page with my e-mail address...

Step 4. Gallery - Portfolio

I spent a lot of time on this page. First of all, I inserted a gallery with a slider. But I thought it wasn't convenient to click on the link inside the gallery in oder to go to the project page in question. So, I realized an easier gallery with the title of each project and a picture of it. So, if you click on the picture, you will go directly to the project page. I like also the fancy gallery, so I inserted one in these project pages. There is a zoom of the picture. You can also click on the play button and each picture is scrolling.

Step 5. Project page

Here is an example of a project page. In order to be clear, I described the different steps of each project. So, there are different boxes with an associated picture to illustrate the text. You can see the procedure of events. At present, there are 4 current projects in this website: TER, Portfolio, Face Pose discrimination and Future Website project.
