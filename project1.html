My fisrt important project was the TER (Travaux encadres de recherche), which means "supervised research work". So, the main goal was to find a subject of research, determine the problem and the hypothesis and then present our own results. In order to be clear, I will decribe our project step by step. I said "our project" because we were 3 people in the team.

Step 1. In quest of the theme and the problem

After many hours of research, we decided to work on - Music and Cognitive Disorders. Music is a complex structure, which doesn't have specific biological functions.However, the music would have beneficial effects on people.

What's why, we were asking us about How can music mitigate some cognitive disorders?

Link to go to: Music and Cognitive Disorders website

Step 2. Find some documentation and establish a plan

Fisrt of all, we went to the library of social and human science to borrow some books. We found aslo some interesting online papers (from "The arts in psychoterapy", "The journal of cogniitve neuroscience"). Then, we decided to interview 3 profesionals: a music therapist, an art therapist and a neuropsychologist, Sir Herve Platel.

I forgot to mention that: We had to create a website in order to show our work. So, we thought about create 4 main parts: music's perception, use music by way of therapy, case study and interviews/videos section.

Step 3. Creation of the website

The fisrt goal was to review Html, Css and Php manual. We created some sketchs in order to organize the structure, and the design. Then we began the design and construction.

Click on the picture above and then on the link at the bottom to go to the website 'Music and Cognitive Disorders'.

Step 4.Presentation

The last step was to present our project in front of an audiance (profeseurs and students) using a power point presentation. We had to speak during 30 minutes: the respect of time was an important criteria.
